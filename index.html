Painterly / Randomized Stroke Bake Filter

Upload a baked image (albedo, mask, normal map, etc.) and convert it into a blocky, painterly texture with randomized strokes. Designed for quickly generating stylized paint layers for 3D materials and texture bakes.

Painterly Stroke Filter

Posterizes colors and re-renders the image using thousands of randomized rectangular brush dabs, keeping your original color regions but giving them a hand-painted look.

Tip: Use square images (e.g. 1024×1024 bakes) for texture work. The output has a black background by default to blend with dark stone.

Built for blender!!
